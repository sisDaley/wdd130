Overview

Purpose

I am creating a website for health and wellness for women over 50. Health is important and especially for women as they begin to age. The site will contain information and links on exercise, nutrition, and overall wellbeing. I will include thoughts and quotes on the importance of staying healthy for our children and grandchildren. Are you interested in learning more about staying fit?

Audience

My audience are women over 50 and women approaching that age. The site is geared to health-conscious women interested in healthy habits and wellbeing. interested in healthy habits and wellbeing.

Content

Home page

Eat well. Exercise Well. Feel Healthy.

Feeling good doesn't have to stop when you turn 50. Women over 50 can have as much energy, stamina, and feel just as good as they did in their 30s and 40s. If you are wanting to take your life back, you've come to the right place. On this site you will find exercises, eating plans, and "feel good" Quotes to elevate your life to the next level and help others do the same.

Ideas for Exercises

Go outside: Kayak, Paddleboard, Hike, Bike, Ski, Rollerblade. These are things women of ANY AGE can do.

Hit the Gym: Weightlifting in proper form is a great way to keep muscles toned, lose fat, stay strong, and have healty bones.

Relax: Yoga, Pilates, Stretching, Meditation. These are great "exercises" for both the body and the mind. Staying flexible is as important as staying strong. Find a buddy and take a class.

Get the family involved: Spend time walking with your husband, Bike ride with your children or grandchildren, Take a class with your sister. Find ways to incorporate exercise into your daily life.

About Me

I am a mother of seven and grandmother to 16. Exercise and healthy eating has been an integral part of my life for many years. Some of my favorite activities are lifting weights, kayaking, taking long walks, and stretching. I follow a macro-based meal plan. The macro formula I currently use is 40% carbs, 30% protein, and 30% fat. I also practice intermittent fasting, which has helped my energy levels. I love to help others with their journeys.
